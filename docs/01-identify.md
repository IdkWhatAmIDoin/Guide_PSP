# 01 — identifying your psp

[← back to main guide](../README.md)

figure out your model and firmware version before doing anything else.

---

## step 1 — find your model number

flip your psp over. the model number is printed on the back label.

| label says | model | nickname |
|---|---|---|
| PSP-100x | psp-1000 | phat / fat |
| PSP-200x | psp-2000 | slim |
| PSP-300x | psp-3000 | brite |
| PSP-N100x | psp go | go |
| PSP-E100x | psp street | street / e1000 |

all models are fully supported by ARK-4, including permanent cfw on all of them.

---

## step 2 — check your firmware version

`settings → system settings → system information`

you need **6.60 or 6.61**. if you're not there yet, go to [updating firmware](02-firmware.md) first.

---

## what's next?

- not on 6.60/6.61 yet → [updating firmware](02-firmware.md)
- already on 6.60/6.61 → [installing ark-4](03-install-cfw.md)
